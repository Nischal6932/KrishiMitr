Field Notes

Before you analyze

Image quality matters

Center one leaf, avoid heavy shadows, and keep the background calm so the model can focus on the lesion pattern.

Context improves guidance

Soil, moisture, and weather do not change the diagnosis directly, but they do improve the irrigation and advisory output.

Confidence is now stricter

The app will flag uncertain results instead of overstating weak predictions, which makes the final answer more trustworthy.
